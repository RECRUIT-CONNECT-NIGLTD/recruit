Blessing Nnenna Okorie

Co-Founder & Lead HR Strategist

Blessing Okorie is a Human Resources professional with over five years of experience supporting organizations in building strong HR systems and fostering productive workplace cultures. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics (Second Class Upper Division) from Ambrose Alli University and is currently pursuing an MBA with a specialization in Human Resources at Ahmadu Bello University.

She began her professional career in administration, serving as an Administrative Manager for three years before transitioning fully into Human Resources. Over the years, she has developed expertise in recruitment, onboarding, employee relations, and HR operations.

Blessing holds the globally recognized Professional in Human Resources – International (PHRi) certification and has completed several HR development courses on Coursera. She is passionate about creating structured HR processes, enhancing employee experience, and helping organizations build engaged and high-performing teams.
